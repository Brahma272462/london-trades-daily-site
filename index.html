Questions

Things you might want to know

Where does the data come from?

Directly from Companies House, the official UK company register.

How often is it updated?

Every weekday morning. You receive new incorporations from the previous business day.

Is it only London companies?

Yes. We filter to high-confidence London postcodes only. Companies registered at service addresses outside London are excluded.

What trades are included?

Construction and building trades: plumbing, electrical, roofing, building, civil engineering, site preparation, scaffolding, and more.

Can I cancel?

Yes, cancel anytime with one click. No contract, no questions.

Do you provide contact details?

The feed includes company name, director name, registered address, trade category, incorporation date, and a direct Companies House profile link. Companies House does not publish email addresses or phone numbers, but many firms use the registered address and director name to send introductory letters.
